ABOUT US

GENERAL INFO
←
→

The **Sierra Leone Football Agency (SLFA)** is the official body overseeing and promoting professional football within the nation.

Our mission is to **develop local talent**, manage national league competitions, and elevate Sierra Leonean football globally.

We are committed to **transparency** and growth at all levels of the sport.

AGENCY PARTNERS
←
→

We work closely with key local and international **sponsors and partners** to fund development projects and leagues.

Our partners include major corporations dedicated to **youth sports development** and community outreach programs.

Partnerships are vital to ensuring the **long-term sustainability** of the Sierra Leonean Premier League.

Visit the official **SLFA Store** for authentic national team kits, training gear, and fan merchandise.

All proceeds directly support **grassroots football programs** and training academies across the country.

Show your support for the Leone Stars and the **future of Sierra Leone football**!

AGENCY HISTORY
←
→

Founded in **1967**, the SLFA has guided the country through decades of football evolution and milestones.

Key historical achievements include **multiple AFCON qualifications** and major regional tournament victories.

We continue to build on this legacy, aiming for **greater international representation** in the coming years.
